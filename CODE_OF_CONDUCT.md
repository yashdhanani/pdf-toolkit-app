# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for all contributors and community members, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Our Standards

We ask that all participants in our community uphold these standards:

- **Be Respectful**: Treat all people with respect and dignity.
- **Be Inclusive**: Welcome people of all backgrounds and experiences.
- **Be Constructive**: Offer helpful feedback and suggestions.
- **Be Collaborative**: Work together toward shared goals.
- **Be Professional**: Use appropriate language and tone in all interactions.
- **Be Patient**: Help others learn and grow.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, bullying, or discrimination of any kind.
- Offensive comments related to gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.
- Unwelcome sexual advances or attention.
- Threats of violence or violent language.
- Spam or trolling.
- Deliberate intimidation or stalking.
- Disruption of discussions or events.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct. Any instances of unacceptable behavior should be reported to the project maintainers.

**Consequences** for violating this Code of Conduct may include:

- A warning or temporary ban from the repository.
- Removal of comments or contributions.
- Permanent removal from the project and community.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by contacting the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and handled appropriately and confidentially.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project repositories
- Project events and discussions
- Community interactions on GitHub
- Social media and other online platforms
